I bought this 2009 BMW 135i from the original owner and converted it into a track-only car. It has about 100,000 miles, weighs 2,920 lbs, and last saw a track in November 2024 on the blown N54. I completed the L92 swap in October 2025. The car is tuned and has done several track weekends since.

<!--more-->

![L92 engine bay with 3D-printed ducting](/assets/images/135i/engine-photo.jpg){:.border.rounded}
*L92 in the 135i bay — heat-wrapped intake, 3D-printed ducting, catch can plumbing.*

## Drivetrain

Six-speed manual with under 40,000 miles, new driveshaft bearing, and a refreshed clutch package — factory dual-mass flywheel, BMW clutch, pilot bearing, and master/slave cylinders. Wavetrac LSD installed in 2020. LSE9X engine and transmission mounts for the swap.

## Engine

The N54 had spun a rod bearing. It had index 12 injectors, a baffled oil pan, and a full refresh roughly a year before failure — factory turbos, rod bearings, gaskets, coil packs, spark plugs, and a new power steering cooler. Full records came with the car.

The L92 uses DOD and VVT deletes, a Summit 8711R3 cam, LS3 intake, and LSE9X long-tube headers. Haltech Rebel LS ECU with an ESP32 CAN bus translator for BMW chassis integration.

## Cooling

Larger front-mount intercooler and CSF radiator. A/C deleted. The dual parallel oil coolers were replaced with a single E90 M3 oil cooler and 3D-printed ducting routed for the LS. Moroso Accusump with electric valve, water injection (tank in trunk). Berk street exhaust.

## Interior

Mostly gutted, sunroof deleted. Factory dash retained. An ESP32-powered Pip-Boy warning light handles alerts. Antigravity lithium battery.

## Suspension

MCS 2WNR two-way adjustable dampers (2022). 650 lb/in front and 900 lb/in rear Hypercoil springs. Front: 60 mm helper springs, Torrington bearings, Turner upper control arms and bushings, Vorshlag camber plates, M3 strut tower braces, upgraded sway bar links. Rear: Ground Control articulating spring perches, M3 subframe bushings, M3 camber link conversion, adjustable camber and toe arms, UUC anti-roll bar.

## Wheels and safety

Clenched fender flares clearing up to 10.5" wheels. Square setup: Apex Arc-8 17×10 with 275/40 NT01 tires. Autopower bolt-in roll bar and harness bar.
